da Vinci Surgical Systems: By the Numbers

The da Vinci surgical system gives your surgeon an advanced set of instruments to use in performing robotic-assisted minimally invasive surgery. The term "robotic" often misleads people. Robots don't perform surgery. Your surgeon performs surgery with da Vinci by using instruments that he or she guides via a console.

  • 5,000,000 da Vinci patients and counting
  • 43,000 da Vinci trained surgeons
  • 15,000 peer-reviewed publications on da Vinci robotic-assisted surgery
  • 4,409+ da Vinci Systems in hospitals worldwide
  • 4,380+ employees
  • 2,700 patents with 1,900 more pending
  • Every 36 seconds a surgeon starts a da Vinci procedure
  • All of these numbers add up to 1 surgeon working on improving lives 1 patient at a time
We strive to make surgery more effective, less invasive, and easier on surgeons, patients, and their families.
